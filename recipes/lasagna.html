Pinoy Cheesy Lasagna

This lasagna is perfect for pasta and cheese lover. Enjoy the cheezy and creamy Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 6 PIECES LASAGNA NOODLES
  • 1 PIECE ONION (LARGE), CHOPPED
  • 4 CLOVES GARLIC, CHOPPED
  • 1/2 POUND GROUND BEEF
  • 500 GRAMS SPAGHETTI SAUCE
  • 250 GRAMS TOMATO SAUCE
  • 1/2 TEASPOON SALT
  • 1/4 TEASPOON PEPPER
  • 5 TABLESPOONS BUTTER
  • 2 1/2 CUP MILK
  • 1 PIECE EGG
  • 1 PIECE EGG
  • 1 BLOCK EDEN ORIGINAL 160G, GRATED
  • 1 1/2 BLOCKS EDEN MELT SARAP 160G, GRATED

How to cook

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to instructions on packaging.
  2. In a large pan, over medium heat, saut√© chopped onions and garlic then add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Add spaghetti sauce, tomato sauce and simmer for 5 minutes until thickened, season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. To prepare the white sauce, melt 5 tbsp. of butter over low heat in a separate pan. Once fully melted, add 4 tablespoons of flour and stir until smooth.
  5. To prepare the white sauce, melt 5 tbsp. of butter over low heat in a separate pan. Once fully melted, add 4 tbsps of flour and stir until smooth.
  6. Add milk one cup at a time, whisk until smooth. Add grated, Eden Original and egg. Cook for 20 minutes to thicken, stirring constantly. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. To assemble lasagna, spread 1 cup of the meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish and top with lasagna noodles until the layer is full. Then spread 1 cup of white sauce on top and add 1 cup of grated Eden Melt Sarap. Repeat these steps for another 2 layers and cover baking with with foil.
  8. Bake in the pre-heated oven for 30-45 minutes or until cheese has melted. Serve and enjoy!
